# SurfaBabe Wellness — Policies

## Shipping
- Delivery within Vietnam
- Ho Chi Minh City: 1-2 business days
- Major cities (Hanoi, Da Nang, etc.): 2-4 business days
- Other areas: 3-7 business days
- Shipping cost depends on location and order size — ask for a quote
- Orders are typically shipped within 1-2 business days of payment confirmation

## Payment Methods
- Bank transfer (Vietnamese banks)
- MoMo
- Cash on delivery (Ho Chi Minh City area only)
- For other payment methods, please ask

## Returns & Exchanges
- Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
- Products must be unused and in original packaging for full refund
- If you have a skin reaction, contact us immediately — we'll work something out
- Damaged or incorrect items will be replaced at no cost

## Order Minimum
- No minimum order required
- Bulk/wholesale inquiries welcome — contact Ailie directly

## Escalation
- For anything unusual, custom orders, wholesale, or issues the bot can't handle, the bot will escalate to Ailie
- Ailie typically responds within a few hours during business hours (Vietnam time, GMT+7)
