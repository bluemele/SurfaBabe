Frequently Asked Questions

What is tallow and why use it in skincare?

Tallow is rendered beef fat from grass-fed cattle. It's been used for centuries in skincare because it's rich in vitamins A, D, E, K and fatty acids that closely match human skin's natural oils. It absorbs beautifully and deeply nourishes.

Are your products vegan?

Our tallow-based products (cream and active sunscreen) are not vegan as tallow is animal-derived. However, our Cocoa Butter Mineral Sunscreen is 100% plant-based and our cleaning products contain no animal ingredients.

Are your sunscreens reef-safe?

Yes! Both sunscreens use non-nano zinc oxide and are completely reef-safe. They provide broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection. Reapply every 2 hours in direct sun.

Does the surface cleaner kill germs?

Our surface cleaners effectively remove dirt, grease, and grime naturally, but they do not disinfect or sanitize. They're great for everyday cleaning without harsh chemicals.

How do I order?

Just message us on WhatsApp! Tell us what you'd like and we'll take care of the rest. We accept bank transfer (Vietnamese banks), MoMo, and cash on delivery in the HCMC area.

Do you deliver across Vietnam?

Yes! Ho Chi Minh City orders arrive in 1-2 days. Major cities (Hanoi, Da Nang, etc.) take 2-4 days. Other areas 3-7 business days. Shipping costs depend on location and order size.

Can I return products?

Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. Unused products in original packaging qualify for a full refund. If you have a skin reaction, reach out immediately and we'll work something out.

Are your products tested on animals?

Never! SurfaBabe is cruelty-free. All products are free from parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and synthetic fragrances.
