SurfaBabe's STORY

We believe in creating all products that are always Natural, Effective, and Affordable.

Hi, I'm Ailie, the founder of SurfaBabe. My story starts in Hawaii, where I grew up surfing and loving the ocean.

During the pandemic, my family decided to go on a big adventure. We sold everything and lived on a boat, sailing through many beautiful places and learned about different cultures.

We then moved to Vietnam, where I finished my high school studies early at age 16. Throughout our travels, we always tried to live healthy, but we had trouble finding good, natural products that worked well and didn't cost too much.

One day in Vietnam, we made our own natural sunscreen for a school project. We were surprised when it worked better than store-bought ones, even during long surf sessions. This made us want to try making more natural products at home.

The big moment came when I got terrible chemical burns from a laundry detergent we bought at the store. From the burn, I was unable to sleep for many days and it took weeks to heal. That's when I decided to make our Natural Laundry Soap, and SurfaBabe was born!

Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for something?
01What is SurfaBabe's All Natural Mineral Sunscreen?

Our All Natural Sunscreen effectively protects you from the sun's rays without any chemicals, fragrances, preservatives or other unnecessary and harmful additives. Our all natural sunscreen has 4 simple, safe, and biodegradable ingredients making it safe and effective for you, your family, and the planet. Unlike chemical sunscreens that may cause health problems, our natural sunscreen is safe for long-term use and gentle on sensitive skin.

02Is All Natural Sunscreen as effective as chemical sunscreen?

Yes! Our mineral sunscreen uses non-nano zinc oxide which provides broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection. It sits on top of the skin and physically blocks rays rather than absorbing them like chemical sunscreens. Studies show mineral sunscreens are just as effective when applied correctly.

03Is All Natural Sunscreen safe for children?

Yes! Our mineral sunscreen is made with simple, natural ingredients and is gentle enough for children. It contains no harsh chemicals, fragrances, or preservatives. We always recommend a patch test first, especially for young children.

04What's the difference of the 2 Sunscreens?

The main difference is the base ingredient: Tallow vs Cocoa Butter. The Tallow (ACTIVE) is great for active people because it's more water and sweat resistant. The Cocoa Butter (Plant-Based) is for those who prefer plant-based options or are less active.

05Does All Natural Sunscreen clog pores?

No! Non-nano zinc oxide is non-comedogenic, meaning it won't clog your pores. Our sunscreen is lightweight and breathable. Many customers with acne-prone skin have found it works great without causing breakouts.

06What ingredients are in the All Natural Sunscreen?

Our sunscreens use only 4 simple ingredients: non-nano zinc oxide (UV protection), beeswax or cocoa butter (moisture barrier), coconut oil (nourishing base), and vitamin E (antioxidant). That's it — no chemicals, fragrances, preservatives, or fillers.

07How does All Natural Sunscreen differ from chemical sunscreen?

Chemical sunscreens absorb UV rays using synthetic chemicals like oxybenzone and avobenzone, which can be harmful to health and the environment. Our mineral sunscreen sits on top of the skin and physically blocks UV rays using zinc oxide — no absorption into your bloodstream.

08Is All Natural Sunscreen safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Zinc oxide is one of the gentlest UV filters available. It's recommended by dermatologists for sensitive skin. Our sunscreen contains no fragrances, preservatives, or chemical UV filters that typically irritate sensitive skin. We always recommend a patch test first.

09Does All Natural Sunscreen leave a white cast?

There may be a slight white tint when first applied, but it blends in quickly as you rub it into the skin. A little goes a long way — use a small amount and spread evenly for the best results.

10How often should I reapply All Natural Sunscreen?

We recommend reapplying every 2 hours, or immediately after swimming, sweating, or towel-drying. The Tallow (ACTIVE) version is more water-resistant, but reapplication is still important for maximum protection.

11Is All Natural Sunscreen water-resistant?

The Tallow Sunscreen (ACTIVE SPORTS) is designed to be water and sweat resistant, making it great for surfing, swimming, and outdoor activities. The Cocoa Butter version is less water-resistant but still offers excellent protection for daily use.

12Is All Natural Sunscreen reef-safe?

Yes! Both sunscreens are 100% reef-safe and biodegradable. They contain no oxybenzone, octinoxate, or other chemicals that harm coral reefs and marine life. Perfect for ocean lovers!

13How do you wash off/take off All-Natural Sunscreen?

Our sunscreen washes off easily with warm water and a gentle cleanser. Since it sits on top of the skin rather than absorbing in, it comes off much easier than chemical sunscreens.

14What is the SPF for All Natural Sunscreen?

Our sunscreens provide broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection equivalent to approximately SPF 30-50, depending on application thickness. Non-nano zinc oxide is one of the most effective natural UV blockers available.

15Can All Natural Sunscreen expire?

Our sunscreen has a shelf life of approximately 12 months from the date of production. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. The natural ingredients are preservative-free, so use within the recommended timeframe for best results.

01What is SurfaBabe's All Natural Laundry Detergent?

Our laundry detergent is made from simple, natural ingredients — no harsh chemicals, sulfates, phosphates, or synthetic fragrances. It's highly concentrated so a little goes a long way. One 1kg bag lasts 65+ loads.

02Why doesn't the All Natural Laundry Detergent create bubbles? Is it still cleaning effectively?

Unlike chemical detergents that use foaming agents (SLS/SLES), our detergent works without creating suds. The cleaning power comes from the natural ingredients, not bubbles. It effectively removes dirt, stains, and odors — the lack of foam actually means it rinses cleaner!

03Is All Natural Laundry Detergent safe for baby clothes?

Yes! Our detergent is free from harsh chemicals, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances. It's gentle enough for baby clothes and sensitive skin. Many parents in our community use it exclusively for their babies' laundry.

04How does All Natural Laundry Detergent differ from chemical laundry detergents?

Chemical detergents use synthetic surfactants, fragrances, phosphates, and optical brighteners that can irritate skin and harm the environment. Our detergent uses only natural cleaning agents that are biodegradable, gentle on skin, and safe for waterways.

05Is All Natural Laundry Soap as effective as regular detergent?

Yes! Our concentrated formula effectively removes everyday dirt, stains, and odors. For heavily soiled items, pre-treat stains before washing. Many customers tell us it cleans better than their previous chemical detergent.

06What ingredients are in All Natural Laundry Detergent?

Our laundry detergent is made with simple, natural ingredients including sodium bicarbonate, washing soda, and essential oils (in the lavender version). No sulfates, phosphates, optical brighteners, or synthetic fragrances.

07Is All Natural Laundry Detergent safe for sensitive skin?

Absolutely! It's specifically designed to be gentle. No synthetic fragrances, dyes, or harsh chemicals that can irritate sensitive skin. We offer both Lavender (with real essential oil) and Unscented versions.

08Can All Natural Laundry Detergent be used in washing machines?

Yes! Safe for all washing machines including HE (high-efficiency) machines. Just use 1 tablespoon per load. It's low-sudsing, which is actually ideal for modern washing machines.

09Is All Natural Laundry Detergent better for the environment?

Yes! Our detergent is 100% biodegradable and free from chemicals that contaminate waterways. The packaging is minimal and eco-friendly. By choosing natural, you're reducing the chemical load on our planet.

Whipped Tallow Cream is a natural moisturizer made from grass-fed beef tallow, rich in vitamins A, D, E, K and fatty acids that closely match human skin's natural oils. It absorbs beautifully, deeply nourishes, and helps maintain your skin's moisture barrier.

02Is this suitable for sensitive skin or acne-prone skin?

Yes! Tallow's fatty acid profile is very similar to human skin's own sebum, making it exceptionally compatible. It's non-comedogenic and many customers with sensitive or acne-prone skin report great results. We always recommend a patch test first.

03Does the cream have a scent?

Our Whipped Tallow Cream is unscented — no added fragrances or essential oils. It has a very mild, neutral scent that fades quickly after application. Perfect for those sensitive to fragrances.

04How do I use the Whipped Tallow Cream?

Apply a small amount to clean, dry skin. A little goes a long way! It warms up with your body heat and absorbs beautifully. Use morning and evening as your daily moisturizer. Can be used on face, hands, body — anywhere that needs hydration.

05Can I use this on my baby or children?

Yes! The simple, natural ingredients make it gentle enough for babies and children. It's free from chemicals, fragrances, and preservatives. Many parents use it for their little ones' dry skin, eczema-prone areas, and diaper rash prevention.

06How long does the 30ml jar last?

A 30ml jar typically lasts 4-6 weeks with daily use on the face. Since the cream is highly concentrated — a little goes a long way! Just a pea-sized amount is enough for your entire face.

07What is the shelf life of the Whipped Tallow Cream?

The cream has a shelf life of approximately 6-12 months. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. The natural vitamin E acts as a preservative. If the texture or smell changes significantly, it's time for a fresh jar.

08Can it be used for specific skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis?

Many customers report improvements in dry skin conditions when using tallow cream regularly. While we don't make medical claims, tallow's vitamins and fatty acids deeply nourish the skin barrier. Consult your dermatologist for specific conditions.

01What is SurfaBabe's Surface Cleaner?

Our Surface Cleaner is an all-natural, powder-based cleaning solution made from simple, biodegradable ingredients. Mix with water to create an effective everyday cleaning spray for your home. Available in Lavender and Unscented.

02What surfaces can I safely clean with this product?

It's safe for most household surfaces including countertops, tiles, glass, stainless steel, wood, and bathroom fixtures. Avoid using on marble, natural stone, or other acid-sensitive surfaces as the alkaline formula may cause damage.

03How do I use the Surface Cleaner?

Mix 1 teaspoon of powder per 1 liter of water in a spray bottle. Shake well before each use. Spray on surfaces and wipe clean. One 1kg bag makes 65+ liters of cleaning solution.

04Is it safe for homes with babies or pets?

Yes! Our cleaner is made from natural, non-toxic ingredients. No harsh chemicals, chlorine, ammonia, or synthetic fragrances. Safe to use around babies, children, and pets. Always rinse food-contact surfaces after cleaning.

05Does the cleaner have a scent?

We offer two versions: Lavender (lightly scented with real lavender essential oil for a fresh, natural fragrance) and Unscented (completely fragrance-free, perfect for those sensitive to scents).

06How long does the 1kg bag last?

One 1kg bag makes 65+ liters of cleaning solution. For a typical household, this can last 6-12 months depending on how often you clean. It's highly concentrated and very economical.

07How should I store the Surface Cleaner?

Store the powder in a cool, dry place away from moisture. Keep the bag sealed when not in use. The mixed solution in your spray bottle stays effective for about 2-4 weeks — make fresh batches for best results.

08Does it Disinfect?

It does NOT disinfect surfaces, but cleans effectively against dirt, grease, and grime. It has a pH of about 9-10 when mixed, making it alkaline and effective for everyday cleaning. For disinfecting, use a separate product.
